Practice Questions Reasoning & Application
  1. Give an example of shared intentionality in everyday life.
  2. Why are humans better at social cognition than other species?
  3. How does understanding intentions help predict behaviour?
  4. Why is group selection considered limited as a theory?
  5. Explain why kin selection helps altruism survive.
  6. How does reciprocal altruism explain helping strangers?
  7. In the Prisoner's Dilemma, why is "defecting" often chosen?
  8. Why can cooperation still emerge even if selfish behaviour exists?
